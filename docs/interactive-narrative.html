The Elevator Ritual

Project Description

Trapped within the claustrophobic confines of an ordinary elevator, the player must navigate a strict, rule-bound urban legend where a single misstep can unravel their reality. By manipulating hidden state flags and constrained choices, the experience is designed to simulate a creeping sense of anxiety and isolation. The narrative explores how psychological tension can be systematically escalated purely through delayed feedback and the player's own anticipation of what waits on the next floor.

My Contributions

Role: Narrative Designer / Programmer / Visual Designer

  • Engineered a rule-driven interactive horror system replicating the "Elevator Ritual" urban legend using SugarCube's state variables and conditional macros.
  • Implemented ritual-sequence validation mechanics where incorrect input branches alter narrative outcomes and tension pacing.
  • Designed multi-ending structure driven by hidden state flags and progression thresholds.
  • Structured player decision loops to build escalating psychological tension through constrained choice architecture.

Mímisbrunnr IKEA

Project Description

Step into the shoes of Odin, the All-Father, as he embarks on a desperate quest for ultimate wisdom—not at the cosmic roots of Yggdrasil, but deep within the labyrinthine aisles of a modern IKEA. This interactive narrative reimagines classic Norse mythology through a lens of biting black humor and corporate irony. By subverting epic mythological tropes, the game forces the player to navigate flat-packed furniture and endless, inescapable showrooms, replacing ancient, blood-soaked sacrifices with the soul-crushing dread of modern consumerism.

My Contributions

Role: Narrative Designer / Visual Designer

  • Designed atmospheric interactive narrative inspired by Norse mythology using Harlowe passage architecture.
  • Implemented animated UI transitions (rumble, shudder, fade, flicker) to synchronize visual effects with narrative tension beats.
  • Structured branching progression with modular passage nodes to control pacing and thematic escalation.
